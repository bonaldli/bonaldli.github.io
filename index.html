Biography

Dr. Ziyue LI (Bonald) is an assistant professor in the Information System Department, WiSo Faculty, University of Cologne. He is also the Chief Machine Learning Scientist at EWI. His research targets at high-dimensional data mining and deep learning methodologies for real-world spatiotemporal problems. His expertises are tensor analysis, spatiotemporal data, and statistical machine learning. Those methods have been applied to various industries, mainly in smart transportation, as well as in smart manufacturing and multimedia.

He actively collaborates with academics, i.e., Prof. Fugee Tsung from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Prof. Hao Yan from Arizona State University and Prof. Chen Zhang from Tsinghua University, as well as industries, Dr. Rui Zhao, Nokia Bell Labs (Germany), HKSTP and SenseTime (HK).

Open Positions: I am always looking for students who are interested in spatiotemporal and smart mobility problems with strong coding abilities. If matched, we could work together in my affiliated institutions or my collaborating organizations. The funding sources are here.
