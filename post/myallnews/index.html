Walking Through The Time: All The News

Jun 2, 2024 2 min read
  • May 2024: Our paper “ CoSLight: Co-optimizing Collaborator Selection and Decision-making to Enhance Traffic Signal Control” is accepted to SIGKDD 2024! Great job, Jingqing.
  • May 2024: We presented two papers in ICLR 2024 (Vienna, Austria) of using Large Language Models (LLMs) as agents to solve complex tasks.
  • May 2024: We presented one paper in AISTATS 2024 (Valencia, Spain) of using contrastive learning to generate data for unsensored locations citywide!
  • April 2024: Our paper “ X-Light: Cross-City Traffic Signal Control Using Transformer on Transformer as Meta Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learner” is accepted to IJCAI 2024! Great job, Haoyuan.
  • April 2024: Me and my colleagues, Yizhou Wang (Northeastern University) and Kuan-Chuan Peng (Mitsubishi Electric Research Lab), are organizing a workshop “ Anomaly Detection with Foundation Models” in IJCAI 2024.
  • April 2024: Dr. Ziyue Li is featured as the highlighted member in Institute of Industrial and Systems Engineers.
  • Mar 2024: Our paper “ Controlling Large Language Model-based Agents for Large-Scale Decision-Making: An Actor-Critic Approach” is accepted to ICLR 2024! Great job, Bin.
  • Feb 2024: Our paper “ FELight: Fairness-Aware Traffic Signal Control via Sample-Efficient Reinforcement Learning” is accepted to IEEE TKDE! Great job, Xinqi.
  • Feb 2024: Our paper “ A General Scenario-Agnostic Reinforcement Learning for Traffic Signal Control” is accepted to IEEE TITS! Great job, Haoyuan.
  • Feb 2024: Our paper “ Low-Rank Robust Subspace Tensor Clustering for Metro Passenger Flow Modeling” is accepted to INFORMS Journal of Data Science! Great job, Jiuyun.
  • Nov 2023: One paper accepted to AISTATS 2024
  • Oct 2023: Two papers were accepted to ACM SIGSPATIAL 2023
  • Oct 2023: I started to teach the course “Advanced Data Analytics and Deep Learning” for Master Program
  • Oct 2023: I started to serve as the Sub-Council Member in INFORMS QSR
  • Oct 2023: I served as Chair in INFORMS Annual Meeting 2023 (Phoenix, Arizona). The two sessions were “Structure Learning from Heterogenous Data” and “Spatiotemporal Decision Intelligence”.
  • Aug 2023: I served as Chair in IEE CASE 2023 (Phoenix, Arizona), with theme in “Spatiotemporal Decision Intelligence”.
  • Aug 2023: I served as Chair in SIGKDD (Long Beach, CA), with theme in “Graph Neural Network”.
